About BoACA

We are a a volunteer-run, local food initiative based in beautiful Bradford-on-Avon, Wiltshire, UK. Our aim is to supply affordable seasonal fruit and vegetables to Bradford-on-Avonâ€™s residents.

We were originally founded as part of a 5 year national Community Supported Agriculture project with the Royal Society of Wildlife Trusts, the Soil association and the Big Lottery Local Food Grant Fund.

We currently offer pick-your-own and a weekly pick-to-order service.

Pick-to-Order

Do you want locally grown, fresh, seasonal and delicious, chemical free fruit and vegetables?

Yes please!

Please contact us to be added to our mailing list, after which you'll receive a list of all our available crops each Saturday.

Volunteering

Our volunteers are what make us tick! Come join us at one of our Wednesday volunteer mornings (10am until noon), or on a monthly volunteer day (first Sunday of every month, weather permitting).
